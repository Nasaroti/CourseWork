How do I play?

General: Use the W,A,S and D keys to move. You can walk to the edge of an area to move into a new one. Your overall goal is to reach the castle of the dark lord and defeat him. Gather gear and slash enemies on the way!

Combat: When in combat use the W,A,S and D keys to move a certain number of spaces as represented by the boot icon. You can press F to skip a turn. Press SPACE to attack in front of you, ending your turn, and avoid the enemies attacks shown by red box's on the floor.
